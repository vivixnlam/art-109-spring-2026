About Me

Vivian Lam is intrigued by digital media art due to its fast-paced and advanced technology. She was born and raised in California, with her parents having immigrated from Vietnam, and has visited countries such as Australia and Paris. She graduated from Westmont High School and received an Associate Degree in Animation. With her Associate Degree in Animation, her recent knowledge of Adobe apps has allowed her to explore Blender and JavaScript, something she hadn't done before in her art. Her interest stemmed from video games such as MapleStory, Genshin Impact, The Legend of Zelda, and many more. Additionally, she integrated Blender and Javascript into her art. Her goal is to work in the 3D Modeling industry, where she aims to create realistic 3D models. Every day, she looks forward to making new artworks.
